Clean, Elegant Design

All our properties are based on modern architecture principles. Both the interior and exterior are built to be hard-wearing, resistant and durable. We've specifically focused on the weaknesses a lot of modern buildings have - both in the summer and winter seasons. This includes anti-mould/fungal technology, bulletproof thermal insulation and exceptional double glazing on all windows. With us you'll have a home that not only looks great, but is of great quality too.

Student Accommodation

Here are Reesons, we have a special division specifically dedicated to student housing. If you're a student looking for accommodation in town, we can definitely help you out. We offer both shared accommodation and single-suite studios to suit your preferrences. Most of our student flats are in well-connected areas with great public transport links and local amenities. On top of that, you'all also get free 24/7 CCTV security & Super-Fast Fibre Optic Broadband. what more could a student want??

Luxury Apartments

If you're looking for something really exotic, then have a look at our Luxury Apartments. These apartments have been designed to give you that high-end, "posh" feeling. They're obviously the most expensive in our property range, but as with everything else in life, you get what you pay for - it's no exception here. They all come with king-size beds, marble shower rooms & spacious kitchen sections. Customer satisfaction to date is 100%!
